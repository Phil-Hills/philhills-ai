Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 4, 2026

Q-Stream Technologies ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Q-Stream API service (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

1. Information Collection

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you generate an API key, we collect your email address to manage your account options and billing.
  • Usage Data: We monitor API request volume and endpoint usage to enforce rate limits and generate billing statements.

2. Use of Data

We use the collected data for:

  • Providing and maintaining the Q-Stream API service.
  • Notifying you about changes to our service.
  • Billing and payment processing via our third-party provider (Stripe).
  • Detecting and preventing technical issues or fraud.

3. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, including encryption in transit and at rest. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Cloud Platform for infrastructure and Stripe for payment processing. These third parties have access to your Personal Data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.
